Appetizers

Momo (Dumplings)

Description: Steamed dumplings filled with a mix of meat or vegetables, served with spicy chutney.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup minced chicken/vegetables
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tsp ginger paste
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Make a dough using flour and water.
  2. Prepare the filling by mixing meat/vegetables, onion, garlic, ginger, salt, and pepper.
  3. Roll the dough into small circles and add filling in the center.
  4. Fold and seal the edges to form dumplings.
  5. Steam for 15-20 minutes. Serve with chutney.

Classic Burger

Description: Juicy burger with a beef/chicken patty, fresh vegetables, and a delicious sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 1 burger bun
  • 1 beef/chicken patty
  • 1 slice cheese
  • Lettuce, tomato, and onion
  • 1 tbsp mayonnaise/ketchup
  • 1 tsp butter

Instructions:

  1. Grill or fry the patty until cooked.
  2. Toast the burger bun with butter.
  3. Assemble the burger: bun, patty, cheese, lettuce, tomato, and onion.
  4. Add mayonnaise or ketchup as desired.
  5. Serve with fries and enjoy!

Margherita Pizza

Description: A simple yet classic pizza topped with tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and basil.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza base
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and oregano to taste

Instructions:

  1. Spread tomato sauce over the pizza base.
  2. Top with mozzarella cheese and basil leaves.
  3. Drizzle olive oil and sprinkle oregano.
  4. Bake in a preheated oven at 200°C (392°F) for 12-15 minutes.
  5. Slice and serve hot!

White Sauce Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup pasta
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Mixed vegetables (optional)

Instructions:

Cook pasta and set aside. In a pan, melt butter, add flour, and cook for 1 minute. Slowly whisk in milk until thick. Stir in cheese, salt, and pepper. Add pasta and vegetables. Serve warm.

Paneer Chilli

Ingredients:

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 capsicum (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp chili sauce
  • 1 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

Heat oil in a pan. Sauté onions and capsicum. Add soy sauce, chili sauce, and ketchup. Toss in paneer cubes, season with salt and pepper, and cook for 5 minutes. Serve hot.

Noodles

Ingredients:

  • 1 pack noodles
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrot, capsicum, cabbage)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp chili sauce
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

Boil noodles and set aside. Heat oil in a pan, sauté vegetables until crisp. Add soy sauce, chili sauce, and vinegar. Toss in noodles, season with salt and pepper, and stir well. Serve hot.

Desserts

Cup Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions:

Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C). Mix all ingredients in a bowl until smooth. Pour batter into cupcake molds. Bake for 20-25 minutes until golden. Let cool before serving.

Vanilla-Ice-Cream-

Ingredients: Milk, cream, sugar, vanilla extract.

Instructions: Mix ingredients, freeze for 4-6 hours, and serve chilled.

Chocolate cake

Ingredients: Flour, cocoa powder, sugar, eggs, milk, butter.

Instructions: Mix dry and wet ingredients, bake at 350°F (180°C) for 30 minutes.

Chocolate Ice Cream

Ingredients: Milk, cream, cocoa powder, sugar, chocolate chips.

Instructions: Combine ingredients, freeze for 4-6 hours, stir occasionally for texture.
