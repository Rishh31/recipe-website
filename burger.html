Classic Burger

Description: Juicy burger with a beef/chicken patty, fresh vegetables, and a delicious sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 1 burger bun
  • 1 beef/chicken patty
  • 1 slice cheese
  • Lettuce, tomato, and onion
  • 1 tbsp mayonnaise/ketchup
  • 1 tsp butter

Instructions:

  1. Grill or fry the patty until cooked.
  2. Toast the burger bun with butter.
  3. Assemble the burger: bun, patty, cheese, lettuce, tomato, and onion.
  4. Add mayonnaise or ketchup as desired.
  5. Serve with fries and enjoy!
